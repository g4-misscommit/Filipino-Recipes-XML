Sinabawang Manok Recipe

Sinabawang Manok is a beloved dish among many homes. Savory with broth, simple to make, yet hearty and filling for any meal. Enjoy this dish warm, especially during rainy days or when craving comfort food.

Ingredients
  • Chicken Wings
  • Magic Magic Chicken Cube
  • Long Green Beans
  • Chayote
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Fish sauce
Directions
  1. Sauté Aromatics and Tomatoes for a Flavorful Base
  2. Brown the Chicken for Added Depth
  3. Simmer the Chicken with Water
  4. Add Vegetables and Seasonings for a Balanced Taste
  5. Finish the Dish by Adding Greens
  6. Transfer to a Serving Bowl. Serve!
